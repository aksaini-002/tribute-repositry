A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Missile Man of India

A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.

He was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He played a pivotal role in India's civilian space program and military missile development efforts.

He was popularly known as the "Missile Man of India" for his work on the country's missile programs.

Biography

  • A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.
  • He studied physics and aerospace engineering and became a key figure in India's space and missile programs.
  • He served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.
  • He was known for his vision of transforming India into a developed nation.
  • Kalam was a recipient of several prestigious awards.
